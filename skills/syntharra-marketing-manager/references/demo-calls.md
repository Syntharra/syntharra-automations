# Demo Call Transcripts

Two scenarios. Both generated for HVAC but parameterised for any trade industry.
These transcripts are used for: demo video, landing page audio, sales material.

---

## Scenario A — Emergency Call (Friday Night, AC Down)

**Use this when:** You want to show the AI handling pressure and urgency.
**Hook:** "This call came in at 8:47pm on a Friday. Did your voicemail catch it?"
**Outcome shown:** Lead captured, technician alerted, customer reassured — all without a human.

---

### Full Transcript — Scenario A

```
[PHONE RINGS]

AI RECEPTIONIST (Sarah):
  "Thanks for calling Arctic Breeze HVAC, this is Sarah. How can I help you tonight?"

CALLER (stressed, female voice):
  "Hi, yes — my air conditioning just stopped working completely. It's almost 9pm,
  it's 97 degrees outside, and I have my 78-year-old mother here with me. I'm really
  worried. Is there any way someone can come out tonight?"

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Of course, I completely understand — that's an urgent situation and we're going
  to take care of you. Can I start with your name and the address where the unit is?"

CALLER:
  "Yes, it's Linda Marsh, and I'm at 4821 Sycamore Drive in Houston."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Got it, Linda. 4821 Sycamore Drive, Houston. And the best callback number for you?"

CALLER:
  "It's 713-555-0192."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Perfect. Now, can you tell me a little more about what's happening?
  Did the system just shut off suddenly, or was it struggling before it stopped?"

CALLER:
  "It just stopped. One minute it was running, then I heard a loud click
  and now it's blowing warm air. The thermostat says 84 degrees inside."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Okay, that's really helpful. A sudden shutdown with a clicking sound
  could be a tripped breaker or a compressor issue — our tech will know
  exactly what to look for. Do you know if you have a standard split system
  or a package unit?"

CALLER:
  "I honestly don't know. It's on the side of the house, the big metal box."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "That's totally fine, our technician will assess it when they arrive.
  I've flagged this as an emergency call and I'm alerting our on-call
  technician right now. You should receive a call from them within
  20 to 30 minutes confirming an arrival time. Is that okay?"

CALLER:
  "Oh thank goodness. Yes, that's wonderful. Thank you so much."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Of course, Linda. Just to confirm — I have Linda Marsh,
  4821 Sycamore Drive Houston, callback at 713-555-0192,
  emergency AC outage, possible compressor or breaker issue.
  Our tech is being notified right now. You're all set."

CALLER:
  "Perfect. I really appreciate it."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Absolutely. Stay cool as best you can — help is on the way.
  Have a good night, Linda."

[CALL ENDS — Duration: 2 min 14 sec]
```

## Scenario B — New Customer Quote Call (Monday Morning)

**Use this when:** You want to show the AI handling a high-value sales opportunity.
**Hook:** "This caller had $8,000 to spend. Your voicemail didn't get the sale. Sarah did."
**Outcome shown:** Full quote intake, appointment booked, upsell planted — zero humans needed.

---

### Full Transcript — Scenario B

```
[PHONE RINGS]

AI RECEPTIONIST (Sarah):
  "Thanks for calling Arctic Breeze HVAC, this is Sarah. How can I help you today?"

CALLER (relaxed, male voice):
  "Hey Sarah, yeah — I'm looking to get a quote on a full AC replacement.
  My unit is about 18 years old and it's been struggling the last couple of summers.
  I figured it's time to just replace the whole thing."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Absolutely, smart move — 18 years is a good run and a replacement
  will save you a lot on energy bills. I can get you set up for a free
  in-home estimate. Can I get your name and address to start?"

CALLER:
  "Sure, it's Mike Castellano. Address is 9302 Birchwood Lane,
  that's in Scottsdale, Arizona."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Perfect, Mike. 9302 Birchwood Lane, Scottsdale. And a good number to reach you?"

CALLER:
  "Yeah, 480-555-0347."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Got it. A couple of quick questions to make sure we send the right person.
  Do you know the approximate square footage of your home?"

CALLER:
  "About 2,400 square feet."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Great. And is this a single-story or two-story home?"

CALLER:
  "Single story."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Perfect. And are you looking to replace just the outdoor condenser unit,
  or the full system including the air handler inside as well?"

CALLER:
  "I was thinking just the outdoor unit, but what do you recommend
  if the indoor one is also old?"

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "That's a really good question — typically if the indoor unit is
  the same age, replacing both at the same time is more cost-effective
  and gives you a matched system with a full manufacturer warranty.
  Our technician will be able to assess both and give you options
  with pricing for each. Does that sound good?"

CALLER:
  "Yeah, that makes sense. Let's do that."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Excellent. For a free estimate at 9302 Birchwood Lane, I have availability
  this Wednesday between 9am and 12pm, or Thursday afternoon between 1pm and 4pm.
  Which works better for you?"

CALLER:
  "Wednesday morning works great."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "Perfect — I've got you booked for Wednesday morning. You'll get a text
  confirmation shortly and a reminder the night before. Our technician
  will assess both units and give you a full written quote on the spot.
  No pressure, just options."

CALLER:
  "Sounds great. Really appreciate it."

AI RECEPTIONIST:
  "My pleasure, Mike. We'll see you Wednesday. Have a great day!"

[CALL ENDS — Duration: 2 min 51 sec]
```
