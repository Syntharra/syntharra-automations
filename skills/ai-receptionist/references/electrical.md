## Emergency Scenarios

IMMEDIATE DANGER (tell caller to call 911 first):
- "Sparks", "fire", "burning smell from outlet/panel" -> evacuate, call 911, then call us
- "Electrical shock", "someone got shocked" -> call 911 if injured

URGENT (same-day service):
- "No power", "power outage" (not utility-wide), "half my house has no power"
- "Exposed wires", "wire hanging down"
- "Buzzing from panel", "hot outlet", "warm wall"
- "Water near electrical", "flooded basement with electrical"
- "Tripping breakers repeatedly"

NON-URGENT:
- Outlet not working, dimming lights, adding outlets, fixture replacement

## Hot Lead Signals (Score 8-10)
- Panel upgrade needed (aging home, frequent tripping)
- EV charger installation (new EV purchase)
- Full home rewiring (renovation, old aluminum wiring)
- Generator installation (power outage concerns)
- Commercial electrical work (higher ticket)
- New construction wiring
- "How soon can you come?" / immediate need

## Warm Lead Signals (Score 5-7)
- Want an estimate for lighting or outlet work
- Asking about smart home installation
- Ceiling fan or fixture installation
- Asking about permits and code compliance
- Recurring issue they want diagnosed

## Cold Signals (Score 1-4)
- Utility power outage (not our problem)
- Asking about their bill
- Wrong number
- Job applications
- Price shopping with no intent

## Seasonal Notes
- **Spring/Summer**: Landscape lighting, pool/spa wiring, outdoor electrical, AC circuit additions
- **Fall**: Generator installs spike (storm season prep), panel upgrades before winter
- **Winter**: Emergency calls for power issues, holiday lighting circuits, space heater overloads
- **Year-round**: EV charger demand growing steadily, smart home installs
