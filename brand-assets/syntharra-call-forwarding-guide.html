Getting Started — Call Forwarding Setup
Forward your existing business phone to your dedicated AI number. Takes under 2 minutes — choose whichever method suits your setup.
iPhone (iOS)
1
Open Settings
Tap the Settings icon on your home screen.
2
Go to Phone → Call Forwarding
Scroll down and tap Phone, then tap Call Forwarding.
3
Enable Call Forwarding
Toggle Call Forwarding to ON (green).
4
Enter your AI number
Tap "Forward To" and type your dedicated AI Receptionist number.
5
Done
Return to the previous screen. To cancel, toggle it OFF here.
Quick Setup — Scan with iPhone Camera
Opens Settings → Call Forwarding directly. Toggle on, then enter your AI number.
Android
1
Open the Phone app
Tap the Phone/dialler icon on your home screen.
2
Open Settings
Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right), then tap Settings.
3
Find Call Forwarding
Tap Calls → Call Forwarding. Samsung: Supplementary Services → Call Forwarding.
4
Select Always Forward
Tap "Always Forward" and enter your AI Receptionist number.
5
Confirm
Tap Enable or Turn On. A confirmation will appear when forwarding is active.

Frequently Asked Questions
Answers to the most common questions from new clients.
General
What if I haven't set up call forwarding yet?
Your calls go to your normal phone or voicemail as usual. The AI only answers calls that are forwarded to your AI number.
Will customers know they're speaking to an AI?
Your AI sounds natural and professional. You can choose to have it disclose it's an AI, or simply position it as your receptionist. Most customers respond well.
What if my AI can't answer something?
It politely takes the caller's details and lets them know someone will follow up. You'll still receive the full call summary.
Can the AI handle multiple calls at once?
Yes — unlimited simultaneous calls. No busy signals, no queues, no missed calls — ever.
Calls & Notifications
Where do missed calls go?
There are no missed calls. Your AI answers every inbound call 24/7/365 — including holidays.
What does the call summary email include?
Caller name, phone number, date and time, job type, property address, urgency level, and key details from the conversation.
How quickly do I get notified after a call?
Typically within 30–60 seconds of the call ending.
Can I add more emails to receive summaries?
Yes — up to 3 email addresses and 3 SMS numbers. Email support@syntharra.com with the addresses to add.
Call Forwarding
Do I need to keep call forwarding on all the time?
Yes, while you want the AI answering calls. To take calls yourself at certain times, use conditional forwarding (forward only when unanswered) via the carrier codes in Section 1.
Will forwarding affect my voicemail?
Unconditional forwarding bypasses your carrier voicemail — your AI answers instead. To keep voicemail as a fallback, use the 'forward when unanswered' codes.
What does call forwarding cost?
Forwarding is typically free or very low cost on most plans. Check with your carrier if unsure.
Can I pause forwarding temporarily?
Yes — cancel using the carrier code (*73 on most carriers, ##21# on GSM) or toggle it off in phone settings. Reactivate any time.

Call Summaries & Notifications
After every call, Syntharra sends you a full summary so you can follow up intelligently.

What's in Each Summary Email

  • Caller name and phone number
  • Property address (if provided)
  • Job type and service category
  • Urgency level (Emergency / Urgent / Standard)
  • Key details from the conversation
  • Notable requests volunteered by the caller
  • Date, time, and call duration

Weekly Report

Every Sunday at 6pm in your local timezone, you'll receive a weekly summary covering the past 7 days — total calls, job type breakdown, and top service requests.

Usage alerts: You'll receive an email at 80% of your monthly allocation and again at 100%. Your AI never stops answering — calls continue even after your allocation is reached.
