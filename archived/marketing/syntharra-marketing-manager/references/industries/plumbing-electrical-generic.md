# Plumbing Industry Profile — Marketing

## Business Profile

**Who they are:** Plumbing contractors, 1–15 employees, owner-operated.
Often on call 24/7 — emergencies are common and high-urgency.
Job range: $200 drain clear → $20,000 pipe replacement.
Year-round demand (no seasonality like HVAC).

**Biggest pain points:**
1. Emergency calls at all hours — physically can't answer everything
2. Losing high-ticket jobs (bathroom remodel, full replumb) to competitors
3. Juggling dispatch + customer calls while on a job
4. Customers who won't leave voicemails on emergencies

**Decision makers:** Same as HVAC — owner/operator

---

## Hook Angles

| Angle | Hook |
|---|---|
| Emergency | "3am. Pipe burst. Baby at home. Did your voicemail handle it?" |
| Lost revenue | "A burst pipe call is worth $2,000–$8,000. Did yours go to voicemail?" |
| Competitor advantage | "The homeowner called 3 plumbers at midnight. Only one answered." |

## Cold Email Subject Lines

1. `3am. Burst pipe. Baby in the house. Did you answer?`
2. `The $6,000 plumbing job that almost went to voicemail`
3. `How {{city}} plumbers never miss an emergency call`

## Emergency Demo Scenario

- Time: 3:12am Sunday
- Situation: Pipe burst in kitchen, water flooding, child in the house
- Urgency: CRITICAL — customer threatening to call anyone who answers
- AI outcome: Caller calmed, plumber dispatched, property damage minimised
- Hook: "They called 4 plumbers. Yours was the only one that answered."

## Quote Demo Scenario

- Time: Tuesday 10am
- Situation: Homeowner wants bathroom renovation quote (full replumb + new fixtures)
- Estimated value: $8,000–$15,000
- AI outcome: Site visit booked, scope captured, upsell to full renovation discussed

## Top Plumbing Markets

Houston TX, Los Angeles CA, Chicago IL, Phoenix AZ, Dallas TX,
San Antonio TX, New York NY, Miami FL, Seattle WA, Denver CO

# Electrical Industry Profile — Marketing

## Business Profile

**Who they are:** Electrical contractors, 1–20 employees.
Range from residential repair to commercial installation.
Highly regulated — licensing + permits matter.
Job range: $150 outlet fix → $50,000 commercial panel installation.

**Biggest pain points:**
1. Missing EV charger / solar enquiries (huge growth market)
2. Commercial clients want fast responses — lose to bigger companies
3. Emergency calls (sparking outlets, power outages) missed after hours
4. Quoting jobs while on site — can't answer phone

**Decision makers:** Owner/operator, sometimes office manager

---

## Hook Angles

| Angle | Hook |
|---|---|
| EV charger boom | "EV charger installs are up 400%. Are you capturing them all?" |
| Emergency | "Sparking outlet at 10pm Sunday. Your competitor answered." |
| Commercial | "That commercial client called 3 electricians. Only one got back to them in time." |

## Cold Email Subject Lines

1. `Sparking outlet. 10pm Sunday. Did your phone answer?`
2. `EV charger installs are booming. Are you missing the calls?`
3. `The $12,000 panel upgrade that almost went to your competitor`

## Emergency Demo Scenario

- Time: 10:15pm Sunday
- Situation: Burning smell from outlet, flickering lights — potential fire hazard
- Urgency: HIGH — safety risk, customer scared
- AI outcome: Safety guidance provided, emergency electrician alerted, job booked

## Quote Demo Scenario

- Time: Monday 8:30am
- Situation: New homeowner wants EV charger installed + panel assessment
- Estimated value: $2,500–$6,000
- AI outcome: Free estimate booked, address captured, licence/permit question handled

## Top Electrical Markets

Los Angeles CA, New York NY, Chicago IL, Houston TX, Phoenix AZ,
San Francisco CA (EV capital), Seattle WA, Boston MA, Miami FL, Denver CO

# Generic Trade Business Profile

Use this when industry isn't HVAC, Plumbing, or Electrical.

## Adapt These Fields

| Field | Replace With |
|---|---|
| Emergency scenario | Industry's most urgent call type |
| Quote scenario | Industry's highest-ticket job type |
| Pain points | What makes this trade owner miss calls? |
| Hook angles | What costs them money when they don't answer? |
| Top markets | Where is this trade most concentrated? |
| Pricing reference | Typical job value range for the industry |

## Universal Hook That Works For Any Trade

"Every call you miss is a job your competitor got."

This works for: landscaping, pest control, roofing, painting, cleaning, tiling, flooring, windows, fencing, garage doors, pool services, tree services, appliance repair.

## Universal Demo Setup

- Call type: After-hours emergency or high-value quote
- AI handles: Caller greeting, issue capture, appointment booking, internal alert
- Outcome shown: Lead captured, job value estimated, zero human involvement

## Universal Cold Email Subject

`The {{dollar_amount}} {{service}} job that almost went to voicemail`

Fill in with highest-ticket job type for that industry.
