# Batch Content

Generate an entire week of content in one session. Use this when you want to front-load the week's content production.

## What to Produce

### 2 Blog Articles
- Read `content/calendar.md` for assigned topics
- If no calendar exists, pick from the keyword target list in the Content Engine skill
- Save to `content/blog/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`
- Each article: 800-1,200 words, SEO-optimized, with CTA

### 5 Social Posts (minimum)
- Mix of Facebook, LinkedIn, and X
- Follow the content mix: 1 pain point, 1 stat, 1 tip, 1 scenario, 1 proof/result
- Save to `content/social/YYYY-MM-DD-platform.md`

### 3-5 Video Scripts
- Follow the Video Content skill format exactly (hook, body, CTA, captions, post copy)
- Mix of formats: at least 1 talking head, 1 screen recording, 1 text-on-screen
- Save to `content/video/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`

### Newsletter Draft (if scheduled this week)
- Save to `content/email/newsletter-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

## After Generation

Update `content/calendar.md` with what was produced and what still needs Dan's action (recording videos, approving posts).

## Quality Check

Before saving each piece, verify:
- One CTA only
- No pricing mentioned
- Brand voice is correct (professional but human, specific, no buzzwords)
- Blog has target keyword in title, first paragraph, and at least 2 subheadings
- Video scripts have clear on-screen text for every beat
- Social posts are platform-appropriate (length, hashtag style, link placement)
