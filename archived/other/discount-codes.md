> **Internal Use Only | Confidential**

## How It Works

Codes are entered by the customer at checkout on the Syntharra pricing page. Each code is single-use per customer and applies a fixed amount off the total. Subscription pricing remains unchanged — only the setup fee is discounted.

Share the appropriate code with the customer via email or during a call. Do not post codes publicly.
