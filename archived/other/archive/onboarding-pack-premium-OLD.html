❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to do anything other than set up call forwarding?
That's it for calls. For integrations, you'll receive a separate email with one-click authorisation links for Google Calendar and {{crm_platform}}. The whole process takes about 3 minutes.
Can I request live transfer for any call?
Yes — any caller can ask to be transferred directly to you. Your AI will call your live transfer number at {{transfer_phone_number}} instantly. You can also request changes to when live transfers are triggered.
What happens if I go over 1,000 minutes?
We'll send you an alert at 80% and 100% usage. Your AI never stops answering — overages are billed at a low per-minute rate at the end of the billing cycle. No interruptions, ever.
How does repeat caller recognition work?
Your AI recognises returning callers by their phone number and greets them by name. Their call history is referenced to give a personalised experience and more relevant responses every time they call.
Can I update the information my AI knows?
Yes. Email support@syntharra.com any time you want to update pricing, services, service area, or anything else. We'll update your agent within 24 hours.
