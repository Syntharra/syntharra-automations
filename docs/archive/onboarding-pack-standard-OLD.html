📞 How to Set Up Call Forwarding

To make your AI Receptionist answer your calls, you need to forward your business number to your AI phone number above. This takes under 2 minutes on most carriers.

🍎 iPhone (iOS)
  1. Open Settings → Phone → Call Forwarding
  2. Toggle Call Forwarding ON
  3. Enter your AI phone number: {{ai_phone_number}}
🤖 Android
  1. Open Phone app → tap the ⋮ menu → Settings
  2. Tap Calls → Call Forwarding
  3. Select Always Forward, enter: {{ai_phone_number}}
📟 Universal Carrier Code (any phone)

Dial the following directly from your business phone — replace the number with your AI phone number:

*72{{ai_phone_number_digits}}

To cancel forwarding at any time, dial *73

💡 Tip: If you have a landline or business phone system (VoIP, RingCentral, etc.), contact your provider and ask them to set up "unconditional call forwarding" to {{ai_phone_number}}. It takes them about 5 minutes.
